Artist Spotlight

Aitor Gomez
Bachata Sensual & Dominican Roots

Explore the seamless connection between the soul of the Dominican Republic and the fluid movements of modern Sensual Bachata with Aitor Gomez at Milano Sensual Congress 2026.

"To understand the evolution of Bachata, one must first dance its roots." - Aitor Gomez

In the global Bachata scene, few artists possess the versatility of Aitor Gomez. While many dancers specialize in either the traditional Dominican style or the modern Sensual Bachata, Aitor has carved a unique path by mastering both and finding the hidden threads that connect them.

The Dominican Soul

Aitor's foundation is built on the rhythmic complexity of Dominican Bachata. His footwork is legendary – precise, fast, and deeply musical. He brings the authentic flavor of the island to every movement, emphasizing that the "footwork" isn't just a set of steps, but a conversation with the guitar and the bongo.

Sensual Evolution

At the same time, Aitor is a prominent figure in the Bachata Sensual world. He understands the body mechanics, the isolations, and the connection required for the modern style. His workshops are unique because they don't treat these two styles as separate entities. Instead, he shows how a Sensual isolation can be enriched with traditional rhythm, and how Dominican footwork can transition smoothly into a Sensual flow.

Workshop: Modern Dominican Footwork

At the Milano Sensual Congress 2026, Aitor will lead exclusive workshops focused on his **Modern Dominican** methodology. Participants can expect to dive deep into:
- **Musicality**: Decoding the instruments of Bachata.
- **Dynamic Footwork**: Fast-paced steps with a modern aesthetic.
- **Styling**: Bringing personality and flavor to your social dancing.

Whether you are a fan of the traditional roots or the modern sensual wave, Aitor's classes provide the missing piece of the puzzle, making you a more complete and expressive dancer.

Master the Roots with Aitor

Join his workshops for a transformational experience at the Milano Sensual Congress 2026.
