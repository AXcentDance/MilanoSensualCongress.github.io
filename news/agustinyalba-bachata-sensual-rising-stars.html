Artist Spotlight

Agustin & Alba
Rising Stars & The Future of Bachata

Combining technical contemporary excellence with the heat of Bachata Sensual, Agustin & Alba represent the next generation of global dance masters.

"Daring to redefine the boundaries of expression in Bachata Sensual." - Agustin & Alba

In a world of established legends, Agustin & Alba have quickly risen to become one of the most exciting and talked-about couples in the international Bachata community. Based in Barcelona, the birthplace of the Sensual style, they bring a fresh, youthful energy that is literally transforming the dance floors of Europe and beyond.

The Contemporary Edge

What sets Alba apart is her extensive background in professional contemporary dance. She brings a level of body control, lines, and artistic depth that is rare in the social dance world. When combined with Agustin's natural charisma and technical mastery of Bachata Sensual, the result is a partnership that feels both incredibly modern and deeply rooted in the essence of the dance.

The Future of Bachata Sensual

They are more than just performers; they are the youngest representatives of the next generation of masters. Their style is characterized by extreme fluidity, technical precision, and a unique way of interpreting the newest Bachata hits. They represent a bridge between the founding principles of Bachata Sensual and a future where artistic expression and athletic prowess go hand in hand.

Workshops at Milano Sensual Congress 2026

We are thrilled to announce that Agustin & Alba will be offering 2 exclusive workshops at the Milano Sensual Congress 2026. These sessions are designed to:
- **Evolve Your Connection**: Modern lead and follow techniques.
- **Artistic Styling**: Incorporating contemporary lines into your Bachata.
- **Musical Sensitivity**: Dancing to the nuances of the song.

Don't miss the chance to learn from the future of the industry. Their energy is infectious, and their methodology is tailored for dancers who want to take their Sensual Bachata to a professional level.
