Artist Spotlight

Irene y Tomas:
Pure Bachata Sensual

Representing the authentic methodology of the style's creators, Korke and Judith.

"Connection is not just about physical contact; it's about two souls moving as one." This mantra defines the world-class teaching and performance of Irene and Tomas.

In the global landscape of Bachata Sensual, few couples embody the official methodology as purely as Irene y Tomas. Trained directly by the creators of the style, Korke and Judith, they have ascended to become official ambassadors of the Bachata Sensual brand, carrying the torch of technical excellence and organic connection to every corner of the world.

From Mallorca to the World Stage

Based in the beautiful island of Mallorca, Spain, Irene and Tomas are the founders and CEOs of InfiniT Dance School. Their partnership, spanning over 8 years, has been a journey of discovery and refinement. What started as a shared passion for movement has evolved into one of the most respected teaching duos in the scene today.

Their approach is rooted in the "Roots of Bachata Sensual," focusing on the body mechanics, waves, and isolations that Korke and Judith pioneered in Cádiz. However, Irene and Tomas bring their own unique flavor—a seamless flow and a technical precision that makes their workshops a "must-attend" for any serious social dancer.

The Official Methodology

As official instructors for the Bachata Sensual organization, they don't just teach steps; they teach a language. Their pedagogy emphasizes the safety of the follower and the clarity of the leader, ensuring that the dance remains comfortable, expressive, and deeply connected.

In their workshops, you can expect a deep dive into the interaction dynamics that define the style. They break down complex "waves" and body isolations into accessible components, allowing dancers of all levels to find their own "flow" while respecting the fundamental principles of the genre.

Experience the Purity at Milano Sensual Congress 2026

We are thrilled to welcome Irene and Tomas to the Devero Hotel for the 2026 edition of the Milano Sensual Congress. Their presence promises an immersive experience into the heart of Spanish Bachata.

They will be conducting a series of Technical Workshops, designed to elevate your social dancing to a professional level. Whether you are looking to master the signature Bachata Sensual connection or polish your body movement technique, learning from two of the most influential ambassadors of the style is an opportunity that comes once in a lifetime.

Join us in Milan to witness their breathtaking show and absorb the knowledge that has made them a benchmark for the new generation of Bachata dancers.
