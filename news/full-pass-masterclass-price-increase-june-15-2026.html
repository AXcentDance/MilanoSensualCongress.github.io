If you are planning to join a Bachata Congress, Bachata Event or Bachata Festival in Europe this year, this is the moment to secure your spot. Milano Sensual Congress is a limited-capacity weekend, and the ticket structure is designed in tiers: the earlier you book, the better your price.

What Is the Current Ticket Price?

The Full Pass is now €125 and remains available at this price until July 31 at midnight. The Full Pass is the core congress ticket for dancers who want the complete Milano Sensual Congress experience: workshops, parties, shows and social dancing.

The Masterclass upgrade is now €65. This upgrade requires a valid Full Pass and adds focused high-level training with selected international artists. No further Masterclass price increases are planned.

Full Pass Countdown to July 31

July 31 at midnight is the next confirmed Full Pass price deadline. After that date, the Full Pass price is expected to move into the next tier as the congress gets closer and availability becomes tighter.

This is important because many dancers wait for the final lineup, the travel plan or the friend group confirmation. That is understandable, but for a limited Bachata Workshop and party weekend, the best ticket price is usually available before the last wave of demand arrives.

Last Year Sold Out for Full Passes and Masterclasses

The strongest reason to book early is simple: last year's edition sold out completely for both Full Passes and Masterclasses. That means dancers who waited too long could not simply decide at the last minute and still get the complete congress experience.

For 2026, interest is already focused on the full package: workshops, parties, international artists, Jack & Jill energy, the official hotel and the chance to dance with a strong international crowd in Milan. A sold-out event creates a great atmosphere, but it also means real limits. Once a category is full, it is full.

Who Should Book Before July 31?

  • Dancers who want the complete Full Pass experience: workshops, parties, shows and socials.
  • Dancers who want the Masterclass upgrade at the fixed €65 price and do not want to risk the advanced training selling out again.
  • International dancers planning flights, hotel and transfers for Milano Dance Events 2026.
  • Groups of friends who want to attend the same congress without splitting across different ticket tiers.

Book the Ticket That Matches Your Weekend

If Milano Sensual Congress is already on your calendar, the practical advice is clear: book the Full Pass before the July 31 deadline. The Full Pass is the safest choice for dancers who want the full Bachata weekend, while the Masterclass upgrade is the best option for dancers who want additional high-level training.
