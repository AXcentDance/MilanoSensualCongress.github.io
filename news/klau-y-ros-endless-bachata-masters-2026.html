Dance Journey

Klau y Ros
Endless Bachata Masters

Discover the signature Endless Bachata style of Klau y Ros. Learn about their journey from Poland and Spain to international Bachata masters joining Milano Sensual Congress 2026.

"Endless Bachata is more than just a combination of steps—it is extreme fluidity, intense musical connection, and emotional storytelling." - Klau y Ros

This November, the Milano Sensual Congress 2026 proudly welcomes one of the most dynamic and sought-after Bachata sensual duos in the world: Klau y Ros. Known globally for their signature Endless Bachata methodology, they bring a distinct flavor to the dance floor that has captivated students and performers alike.

The Origins: A Global Journey

Klau (Klaudia Perdek) brings an extensive competitive background. Starting at age eight with ballet and salsa, she holds podium finishes in major championships such as the World Latin Cup and BachatArt. Her extreme technical precision is a hallmark of their style.

Adrián Ros complements this precision with deep structural knowledge. Originating from Galicia, Spain, Ros started with sport dance and Cuban salsa in 2005. By 2014, he was expanding into fundamental styles and later refined his stage presence under top professionals like Marco y Sara in Madrid.

The 'Endless Bachata' Academy

Now based in Orlando, Florida, the duo runs the highly successful Endless Bachata Academy. Their teaching philosophy emphasizes seamless, non-stop movement where the conclusion of one figure effortlessly breathes into the beginning of another.

Their approach strips away chaotic arm-work, favoring deep body isolations, grounded weight transfers, and musicality that connects partners in a profound, uninterrupted flow.
