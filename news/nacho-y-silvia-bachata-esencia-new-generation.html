Artist Spotlight

Nacho y Silvia:
The Evolution of Esencia Style

Meet the Madrid-based duo who are redefining the boundaries of Esencia Style with fresh energy and technical mastery.

"Dance is a conversation between two souls." This belief drives Nacho and Silvia, the rising stars of the global Bachata scene and prominent members of the Esencia Group.

In the vibrant dance studios of Madrid, a new movement is taking shape. At the forefront of this evolution are Nacho y Silvia, a couple whose technical precision and creative flair have marked them as key artists in the modern Bachata scene. As representatives of the elite Esencia Group, they stand alongside legends like Marco y Sara and Gero y Migle, continuing the legacy of deep connection and organic movement.

The Madrid Connection: Rooted in Excellence

Growing up in the epicenter of the Bachata revolution, Nacho and Silvia have been immersed in the most advanced methodologies from the beginning. Their training is a testament to the high standards of the Madrid scene, blending the traditional foundations of Bachata with the modern, fluid transitions of Esencia Style.

Being part of the Esencia Group means more than just sharing a name; it involves a shared commitment to the Esencia methodology. Nacho and Silvia consistently work with the movement's pioneers to refine their understanding of weight transfer, body mechanics, and the psychological connection between partners.

Defining their Artistic Vision

What makes Nacho and Silvia truly special is their ability to bridge the gap between established technique and fresh, contemporary energy. They bring a youthful vitality to their workshops that resonates with dancers of all levels. Their style is characterized by explosive speed balanced with momentary stillness, creating a dynamic rhythm that is both exciting to watch and incredibly fun to dance.

Their impact on the Madrid community has already been significant, and now they are expanding their reach to the global stage, proving that the future of Esencia Style is in very capable hands.

Featured Workshops at Milano Sensual Congress 2026

We are thrilled to welcome Nacho y Silvia to the Devero Hotel for their debut at the Milano Sensual Congress. They will be leading a series of intensive Esencia Style Workshops, designed to help dancers unlock more fluid movements and build a stronger, more intuitive connection with their partners.

This is a rare opportunity to learn the latest evolutions of Esencia Style from the artists who are living and breathing it every day in Madrid. Whether you want to master intricate footwork or understand the subtle nuances of leading and following, Nacho and Silvia's presence in Milan will be a transformative experience for your dance journey.
