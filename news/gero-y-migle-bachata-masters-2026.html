Artist Spotlight

Gero y Migle:
Masters of Esencia Style

Uncovering the journey and unique chemistry of the couple transforming modern Bachata with their signature method.

"Energy is the soul of the dance." This philosophy has carried Gero and Migle from their diverse backgrounds in Spain and Lithuania to the pinnacle of the international Bachata scene.

In the world of Bachata, few couples possess the unique blend of technical discipline and explosive charisma that defines Gero y Migle. While many mistakenly categorize their dance as standard Sensual, Gero and Migle have actually developed their own distinct philosophy and movement language: the Esencia Style. As we prepare for the Milano Sensual Congress 2026, we take an in-depth look at the artists who have become a global benchmark for dancers everywhere.

Esencia Style: Beyond the Labels

Gero and Migle consistently emphasize that they do not follow the standard Bachata Sensual curriculum. Instead, they have fused their extensive backgrounds—from Ballet, Modern, Hip-Hop and more—into what they call Esencia Style. While it shares the fluid, connected movements often associated with modern Bachata, Esencia Style prioritizes the natural weight transfer and organic body mechanics derived from their years of multi-disciplinary training.

Gero, hailing from the vibrant island of Ibiza, Spain, began his pursuit of movement at the age of 14. His early training was a masterclass in versatility, covering Ballet, Modern, Hip-Hop and more. This eclectic base refined his understanding of body isolation and weight transfer—skills he would later perfectly adapt to partner dancing.

Meanwhile, in Lithuania, Migle was carving her own path through folk dance, contemporary, and show dance. Her move to Madrid, the heart of the Bachata revolution, was the catalyst that transformed her technical excellence into the soulful, fluid style she is known for today.

The Gero y Migle Methodology

What sets them apart is not just their breathtaking performances, but their teaching methodology. They are known for their infectious energy and a structured approach that prioritizes comfort, safety, and technical precision.

In an Esencia Style Workshop, students are encouraged to find their own expression within the framework of Gero and Migle's unique methodology. They focus on the intricate lead-and-follow dynamics that allow a couple to feel "weightless" on the floor. It is this dedication to the *feeling* behind the move that has made them one of the most recognized couples in the international dance scene.

Exclusivity at Milano Sensual Congress 2026

For the 2026 edition of our congress, Gero and Migle are bringing a specially curated series of workshops to the Devero Hotel. Beyond their main stage performance—which promises to be a highlight of the weekend—they will be hosting exclusive Esencia Style Masterclasses. These advanced sessions will focus specifically on the roots of their style, breaking down the fundamental body mechanics and philosophical origins that distinguish Esencia from other Bachata methods.

Attendees will have the rare opportunity to learn directly from the source, absorbing the nuances of their style in the premium, luxury environment that defines the Milano experience. Whether you are looking to master body rolls or perfect your connection, their presence in Milan is an opportunity not to be missed.
