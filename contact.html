Frequently Asked Questions

How can I contact the organizers?

You can contact us directly via WhatsApp in Italian/Spanish at +39 333 393 5911 or in English at +41 79 966 84 81 for any specific inquiries not covered on this website.

Where exactly is the event located?

The event takes place entirely at the Devero Hotel in Cavenago di Brianza. This 4-star exclusive venue hosts all workshops, day socials, and night parties.

How do I buy tickets?

Tickets are available through our official partner Lasalsadelbaile.com. You can access the purchase page directly via the "Tickets" link in our navigation menu.

What is the nearest airport?

The venue is strategically located.
• Milan Linate (LIN) is the closest (approx. 20 mins).
• Milan Bergamo Orio al Serio (BGY) is very convenient (approx. 25 mins).
• Milan Malpensa (MXP) is about 45-50 mins away.
