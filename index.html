The Team Behind The Magic

Organizers of the Bachata Congress Italy

Marco y Valeria

Congress Directors

@marcoyvaleria — International Sensual Bachata Artists with dance school established in Arcore, MB. Creators of Milano Sensual Congress and its Spring Edition. With a decade of teaching and performance experience, they are directors of the Gero y Migle Project Performance Show in Milan, and Marco serves as artistic director for one of the top Latin club in Milan, the UnaMas Club.

Ale y Xidan

Congress Directors

@aleyxidan — International Sensual Bachata Artists. They founded AXcent Dance, the first Bachata Sensual specialized school in Zurich, established in 2025. They also serve as judges for the International Swiss Championship for Salsa and Bachata. Ale y Xidan are the creators of Milano Sensual Congress and its Spring Edition with Marco y Valeria, and are the co-organizers of Bailadoro Dance Weekend.

Bachata Congress FAQ

Where is the event located?

The entire event takes place at the New Devero Hotel & Spa (4 Stars Executive), located in Cavenago di Brianza. It is easily accessible from Milan airports.

Can I compete in the Jack & Jill?

Yes! We host an International Jack & Jill competition. Registration details and categories will be announced closer to the event date on our social media and website.

How do I book the hotel?

Sales for the official hotel open in January 2026. Please visit our Hotel Page for the official booking link to get the group rate.

Will there be day socials?

Yes, both Saturday and Sunday will have day socials only accessible for who has the Full Pass (not party pass).

Are the Bachata Masterclasses taught in English?

Yes, all our international workshops, masterclasses, and main stage communications at the Milano Sensual Congress are conducted in English to accommodate our European and global attendees.

Which airport is best for the Milano Sensual Congress?

Milan Bergamo Orio al Serio (BGY) and Milan Linate (LIN) are the closest, but Milan Malpensa (MXP) is also highly convenient. We offer dedicated direct transport shuttles from all three airports to the hotel.
