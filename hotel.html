Hotel & Booking FAQ

When do hotel sales start?

Hotel room sales will officially open in January 2026. We recommend booking as soon as the link goes live, as the main hotel sells out quickly.

What is the official hotel?

The event takes place entirely at the Devero Hotel, a 4-star executive hotel. All workshops, parties, and social activities are under one roof for maximum comfort.

Can I change the name on the room reservation?

Yes, name changes for room reservations are possible. If you need to transfer your room to another attendee, please contact us or the hotel reservation desk (specific instructions will be provided upon booking).
