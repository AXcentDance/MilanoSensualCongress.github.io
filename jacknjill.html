Unlock Your Potential:
International Jack & Jill 2026

The most anticipated Bachata competition in Europe is back. Are you ready to take the stage?

"Real growth happens under the lights." This year, the Milano Sensual Congress is not just hosting an event; we are searching for the next generation of international artists.

The International Jack & Jill at Milano Sensual Congress has always been a highlight of the weekend, but for 2026, we are raising the stakes. This isn't just a competition; it's a bridge between being a talented social dancer and becoming a recognized international artist. Bringing together top dancers from all over Europe, the competition promises to be the most competitive and high-energy session of the entire festival.

A World-Class Panel

To ensure the highest standard of fairness and prestige, our jury is composed entirely of international masters. These are the judges who have shaped the world of Bachata Sensual, looking for the perfect blend of connection, musicality, and technique. Whether you are coming from Spain, France, Germany, or beyond, you will be performing for the people who defined the dance.

Event Details & Registration

Mark your calendars for the grand finale: the competition will take place on Sunday, November 22nd. We believe in making the stage accessible to all true lovers of the dance, which is why the entry fee is kept at only 10 EUR for anyone holding a Full Pass or a Full Party Pass.

Registration is now open exclusively on Salsadelbaile. Spots are strictly limited to ensure a smooth flow for all competitors, so we recommend securing your entry early to avoid missing out on the magic.

The Prizes: Your Future Starts Here

We are looking for more than just a winner—we are looking for a colleague. The top prizes for the International Jack & Jill 2026 include:

  • Cash Prizes: Significant financial rewards to support your international dance journey.
  • Official Artist Slot for 2027: The winner will receive an invitation to join the official artist lineup for the Milano Sensual Congress 2027.
  • Full Passes for Next Year: Secure your luxury experience for our next edition with free Full Passes for the top finalists.

Beyond the prizes, competing on the Milano stage provides unmatched international recognition—giving you bragging rights as a top-tier dancer on Italy's most prestigious Bachata stage.

Join the elite dancers of Europe and prove your skills on the Milano stage. Entry is just 10 EUR.
